A Passion for Railroads

Our mission

Engaging the community through model railroading. The Tennessee Valley Model Railroaders bring together families, hobbyists, and newcomers to celebrate craftsmanship, engineering, and storytelling on the rails.

Who we are

We are a vibrant club dedicated to sharing the joy of model railroading. Our expansive layout captivates visitors while promoting creativity, learning, and community.

Open Saturdays

Visit us every Saturday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Free admission for families and hobbyists, with donations helping us grow the layout and events.

Community focus

We support education, youth interest, and local events through open houses, shows, and collaborative build sessions.

A Passion for Railroads

Our Mission

Engaging the community through model railroading. The Tennessee Valley Model Railroaders bring together families, hobbyists, and newcomers to celebrate craftsmanship, engineering, and storytelling on the rails.

Who We Are

The Tennessee Valley Model Railroad is a vibrant club dedicated to sharing the joy of model railroading. Open every Saturday, we offer free admission and encourage donations to support our activities. Our expansive layout captivates visitors while promoting creativity and learning. We aim to inspire the next generation to embrace this enriching hobby, providing resources and activities that enhance their experiences and ignite their passion.

Our Heritage and Legacy on Rails

We want to take time to immortalize some of the club members who are no longer with us. Their friendship and contributions will never be forgotten. Their craftsmanship, friendship, and dedication are woven into every part of our layout.

To our members who have caught the final train home: your companionship and craftsmanship are forever woven into the very fabric of this layout. High green signals and clear tracks ahead, friends. You are deeply missed but always remembered.
